長者常用藥物

Common Geriatric Medications

Glucophage XR Tab 500mg

藥丸 Tablet

用於 2 型糖尿病

For Type 2 diabetes mellitus.

其他資訊 Other Information

    劑量 Dosage

    最大日劑量 = 2g； 從每天一次 500 毫克早餐開始至少一周，然後每天兩次 500 毫克早餐和晚餐至少一周，然後每天 3 次 500 毫克早餐、午餐和晚餐

    Maximum daily dose = 2g; Start with 500 mg once daily with breakfast for at least a week, followed by 500 mg twice daily with breakfast and dinner for at least a week, and then 500 mg 3 times daily with breakfast, lunch and dinner.

    常見副作用 Common Side Effects

    腹痛、食慾下降、暫時性腹瀉、胃腸道疾病、噁心和嘔吐以及味覺改變

    Abdominal pain, decreased appetite, transient diarrhoea, gastrointestinal disorder, nausea and vomiting, and altered taste.
